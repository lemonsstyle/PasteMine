Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Summary: PasteMine is a strictly local macOS application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers. Your clipboard history remains exclusively on your device.

1 Introduction

PasteMine ("we," "our," or "us") respects the privacy of our users ("user" or "you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our macOS application (the "Application").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2 Interpretation and Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • Application refers to PasteMine, the software program provided by the Developer.
  • Device means any device that can access the Service such as a computer (macOS).
  • Personal Data is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • Local Storage refers to the storage space on your personal Device.
3 Collection of Data

The Application collects data solely for the purpose of providing its core functionality. This data is processed and stored locally on your Device.

Clipboard Data

When you use the "Copy" function (Cmd+C) on your computer, the Application records:

  • Text content (Plain text and Rich text).
  • Image files (PNG, JPEG, TIFF, etc.).
  • Metadata regarding the source application (App Name and Bundle ID).
Usage Settings

We store your preferences locally, such as keyboard shortcuts, sound effect settings, and notification preferences.

4 Data Storage and Security

We adhere to a "Local First" architecture to ensure your data security.

  • Storage Location: All data is stored within the Application's sandbox directory (~/Library/Application Support/PasteMine/).
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not operate any servers. Your data is never uploaded to the cloud or synced across devices by us.
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) to track your usage.
5 User Control and Rights

Since all data is stored locally, you have full ownership and control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all stored history within the Application's main window.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual records or clear the entire history using the "Clear All" function in the Application.
  • Prevention: You can set the Application to ignore specific apps (e.g., Password Managers) or specific data types to prevent them from being recorded.
6 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

3 数据收集

本应用仅为了提供核心功能而处理数据，所有数据均在您的设备本地进行处理和存储。

剪贴板数据

当您在计算机上使用“复制”功能（Cmd+C）时，应用程序会记录：

  • 文本内容（纯文本和富文本）。

我们遵循“本地优先”的架构以确保您的数据安全。

  • 删除权： 您可以删除单条记录，或使用“清空历史”功能删除所有数据。
  • 预防措施： 您可以设置应用程序忽略特定的 App（如密码管理器）或特定的数据类型，以防止它们被记录。
